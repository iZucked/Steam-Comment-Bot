Vessel Class Fields

Name

The name of the vessel class.

Inaccessible Ports

A list of ports which vessels of this class cannot use.

Base Fuel

The type of base fuel used by vessels of this class.

Capacity

The LNG tank volume in m3 for vessels of this class.

Fill Capacity

The percentage of the LNG tank volume which can actually be filled with LNG.

Min / Max Speed

The minimum and maximum travel speed of the vessel in knots.

Min Heel

The minimum remaining quantity of LNG required to keep the vessel operational at the end of a voyage if LNG is on board.  

Warming Time

The amount of time in hours that the vessel can operate with empty LNG tanks before requiring a cooldown.

Cooling Volume

The amount of excess LNG required to cool the tanks when they are warm.

Route Parameters

Information on time and fuel consumption used to travel canals or other unusual routes.

Pilot Light Rate

The amount of base fuel used to keep a vessel operational on a pure LNG boiloff trip.

Min Base Fuel Consumption

Laden / Ballast Attributes

These panels allow you to edit the details for the vessel class’s fuel consumption when the vessel is on a laden (carrying LNG cargo in its tanks) or ballast (travelling without cargo) voyage.

NBO Rate

The amount of LNG boiled off during a laden / ballast voyage

Idle NBO Rate

The amount of LNG boiled off while idling laden / unladen (ballast).

Idle Base Fuel Rate

The amount of base fuel consumed while idling laden / unladen (ballast).

In Port Base Rate

The amount of base fuel consumed while being actively processed at a port under laden / ballast conditions.

Service Speed

The typical voyage speed for a vessel when laden / unladen (ballast).
