Validation

The Validation view displays a list of warning and error messages for the scenario currently being edited.

Note: not to be confused with application errors recorded in the Error Log view.

Tip: Double-click on a validation message to switch to and highlight the relevant data item in the relevant editor tab (note for ports and vessels, etc. switch to the Data perspective).

Warning messages are generally data issues which the optimiser can work around but may lead to suboptimal results. Error messages are generally for gaps or inconsistencies in the input data, or conflicting requirements that the optimiser is unable to fix (by moving slots around), or even evaluate properly.
