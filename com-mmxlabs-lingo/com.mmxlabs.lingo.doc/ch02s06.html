Spot Cargoes

The Spot Cargoes tab defines spot cargo markets for the four types of buy and sell - FOB Purchase, DES Purchase, FOB Sale, and DES Sale. These are automatically generated buy and sell opportunities and can be created in the trades tables wiring menus or by the optimiser. Each market is defined with a number of options available per month (and each option has a month long window). Spot market slots inherit most of their attributes from the spot market itself.

Spot Market Editor

Name

A unique name to identify this spot market.

Min Quantity / Max Quantity

Minimum and maximum volumes (in m3) of LNG for a transaction in this market.

Entity

The legal trading entity which trades in this spot market.

Notional Port

The port at which this market is deemed to operate.

CV

The CV of the gas

Market Ports

A list of ports which this market can be accessed at.

Constant

Number of cargoes per month

Price Expression

A price expression specifying the expected price at which LNG is sold in this market (see price expressions).
